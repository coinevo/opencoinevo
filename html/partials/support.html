What is Open Coinevo?
Open Coinevo is an open source, re-implementation of https://mycoinevo.com/. The frontend, html, css, JavaScript, were adapted from, and originally developed by https://mycoinevo.com/. The Open Coinevo backendis open source, free to use and modify.
